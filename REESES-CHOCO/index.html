THE "REESE'S" STORY

Reese's Peanut Butter Cups have been an American favorite since their invention in 1928 by H.B. Reese, a former dairy farmer. Combining the creamy, nutty richness of peanut butter with the smooth sweetness of milk chocolate, Reese's created a flavor harmony that has captivated taste buds for generations. With their iconic orange packaging and perfect balance of salty and sweet, Reese's has become a cultural phenomenon, inspiring countless variations and recipes. From holiday-themed treats to the classic cup, Reese's continues to be a symbol of comfort, indulgence, and joy for chocolate and peanut butter lovers worldwide.
